COVID-19

What Should We Do?

To prevent infection and to slow the transmission of COVID-19, do the following:

Wear Masks

Masks may be more effective as a “source control” because they can prevent larger expelled droplets from evaporating into smaller droplets that can travel farther.

01

Wash Your Hands

Hand Hygiene is one of the most effective actions you can take to reduce the spread of pathogens and prevent infections, including the COVID-19 virus.

02

Maintain Social Distancing

Social distancing helps limit opportunities to come in contact with contaminated surfaces and infected people outside the home.

03

Avoid Touching Your Face

Touching your face with infected hands can lead to the transmission of the virus into your body. In order to prevent this, It is highly recommended to avoid touching your face.

Cover Your Cough And Sneeze

Cover your mouth and nose with a tissue or your bent elbow when coughing or sneezing to help prevent the transmission of the virus. Immediately throw the tissue in a covered dustbin.
